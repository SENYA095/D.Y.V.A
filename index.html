FAQ

We are always happy to help you.

What is a natal chart?

It’s a personalized map of the planets’ positions at the time of your birth, revealing aspects of your personality and life paths.

How long does the photoshoot take?

The entire process, including preparation and shooting, takes around 3–5 hours.

Do I need to prepare for the shoot?

No, our team will take care of creating the concept, choosing your look, and ensuring the right atmosphere.

Can I get the photos in digital format?

Absolutely! We’ll provide you with professionally edited images in high quality.

What can I do if I don't know the exact time of birth?

It's not a problem, our astropsychologist can select an image even without the exact time of birth.
